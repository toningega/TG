ABOUT ME

Mathematician | Data Analyst | Problem solver

I am a data analyst at a Music Licensing company in Central London working to help them make business decisions, drive efficiency and productivity. My focus is on providing insights on non-standard business processes with the help of data and machine learning methods.

I studied Mathematics at Queen Mary University of London and have been involved in data and technology for roughly 5 years. My personal projects revolve around the continual development of my machine learning and deep learning techniques along with smaller python programming projects for automation. Some of these can be found on my GitHub.

Outside of work I have been teaching all levels of mathematics to students for over 8 years and I am currently developing a course on essential mathematics in the workplace (official name coming soon).

I have seen how a lack of basic mathematics knowledge in the workplace has led to inefficiencies and incorrect calculations resulting in monetary losses. My aim is to enrich people in all fields with the knowledge and confidence to tackle the maths problems they may come across in their day to day.

I also have a few individual clients who I consult with on SQL queries and best practices with automation and data visualisation.
